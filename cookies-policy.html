Cookies Policy

Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Webelizard.com ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (https://webelizard.com). By accessing or using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, etc.) that store information about your interactions with websites. These files are stored in your browser and are used to improve your browsing experience, for example by remembering your preferences, keeping you logged in, and analyzing site traffic.

Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of the website. Without them, certain features of the site may not work properly. These cookies do not collect personal information and are stored only for the duration of your session.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how users interact with our website, such as which pages are visited the most, how long visitors spend on each page, and if they encounter any errors. This helps us improve the performance and user experience of our website.
  • Functional Cookies: Functional cookies are used to remember your preferences and settings, such as language preferences, so that you do not have to re-enter them every time you visit the site. These cookies enhance your user experience but are not essential for the website to function.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: These cookies track your browsing behavior to provide personalized content and advertisements that are more relevant to you. They are often set by third-party advertising networks and are used to monitor the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

How to control cookies

You can control the use of cookies on our website by adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, accept certain types, or delete cookies that have already been placed. Please note that if you disable or delete cookies, some parts of the website may not function properly.

Changes to this Cookies Policy

We may update our Cookies Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookies Policy on this page.
