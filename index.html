Our E-Commerce Website Workflow

1

Website Characterization

Initial Consultation to understand your business goals, target audience, and design preferences. We’ll also discuss essential features such as product listings, payment gateways, and shipping options.

Proposal and Scope outlining deliverables, timelines, and costs to set clear expectations.

2

Design and Development

Design Mockups to present and review. We’ll incorporate feedback to align the design with your brand and user experience goals.

Development phase to build your website with all necessary functionalities including product catalogs, shopping carts, and payment processing systems.

3

Launch and Support

Testing and Quality Assurance to ensure everything works correctly across devices and browsers.

Training on how to manage the site, followed by the official launch and ongoing support for any issues or updates.
